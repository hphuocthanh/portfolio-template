Grocery Store Management

Mini Capstone

In short, this project aims to provide small to medium-sized grocery stores in Vietnam with a management and POS system that encompasses a variety of functionalities.

This is a collaboration work. I designed the interface for POS, handled everything for Product List Mangement page, refactored and made small improvements as the project went on.

For the POS, we want to make it as intuitive as possible. The layout is pretty straightforward, with half left of the screen for product selection and the other half for checkout.

New and regular customers can also gain and apply points for each of their bills. Checkout allows the cashiers to take a final look at the bill before proceeding.

For the Admin Management, we want to make it as informative as possible. The homepage is the dashboard, with the most important information being displayed.

The other pages offer more management operations for the admin (store owners), such as Inventory Management, Import Goods, Receipt List Management, Product List Management, Statistics,...
